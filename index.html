Hydrassistant

ON-DEMAND DEHYDRATION SENSING

retractable mouthpiece

The mouthpiece retracts to protect the sensor and to toggle the device on and off.

instant measurement

Hydrassistant analyzes saliva conductivity to provide immediate dehydration measurements, with severity indicated by multi-color LEDs.

portable design

The processor is small and lightweight, making the Hydrassistant easy to carry and maintain.

patent pending

A patent for the design and functionality of the device is pending approval from the US Patent and Trademark Office.

for athletics

The hydration state of an athlete undergoes constant flux during practice and during competition. Hydrassistant helps athletes be at the top of their game through on-demand hydration status measurements, optimizing performance by avoiding both dehydration and over-drinking.
